    Risk-based Approach in Benign/chronic hematology for Optimization of Workloads

      Business Objective

    Segment and Prioritize SMZs with a deficit of Haematologists as compared to patients for targeting.

      Context

    Due to lack of dedicated HEM clinics and ageing population, the number of patients outweighs Haematologists. This results in delay of care for high risk patients. Current data on the hospitals, HCPs, patients etc. exists in silos and is not streamlined.

      Business Outcome

    Enable all hematology disease patients to receive optimal diagnosis and treatment by supporting hematologists to manage workload with task-sharing approach.

      Approach

    Integrate different data sources like Novartis internal data, DDD, Takumi-San etc. to get complete mapping of Haematologists to Hospitals and Hospitals to SMZs. Cluster SMZs on the basis of the Haematologists to patient ratio and other features. Develop a dashboard with a geo plot depicting the different SMZs and their segmentation for targeting.

      Project Outcome

    Clusternig was done and efficient results were declared to target 9% of SMZ to affect 42% hems and helping 32% patients in Japan
